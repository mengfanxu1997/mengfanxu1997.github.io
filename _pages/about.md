Hi! I am Mengfan, and I am a Tenure-track Assistant Professor in the [Department of Mechanical and Industrial Engineering](https://www.umass.edu/engineering/about/directory?s=&field_person__profile_type_ref_target_id%5B411%5D=411&field_person__related_programs_target_id%5B91%5D=91&field_person__related_programs_target_id%5B156%5D=156&page=0) and an Adjunct Assistant Professor in the [Manning College of Information & Computer Sciences](https://www.cics.umass.edu) at [UMass Amherst](https://www.umass.edu/). 

I obtained my Ph.D. from the [Department of Industrial Engineering and Management Sciences](https://www.mccormick.northwestern.edu/industrial/) at Northwestern University in 2024, where I am very fortunate and grateful to be advised by [Prof. Diego Klabjan](https://dynresmanagement.com/vita). Previously, I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Statistics from [University of Science and Technology of China](https://en.ustc.edu.cn/) in 2018, with a summer exchange program in BioStatistics in the [School of Public Health at Yale University](https://ysph.yale.edu/) in 2017. 

My research group works at the intersection of operations research, machine learning and artificial intelligence, with a particular emphasis on online learning. We formulate real-time decision-making problems under uncertainty and develop algorithms with provable and generalizable performance guarantees. Motivated by operational challenges faced by real-world decision makers, our work develops new methodologies that capture the intricate complex and structures underlying these problems, with applications across a broad range of domains. Our research interests include:

- Online Learning
- Multi-Agent Systems
- Statistical Learning and Algorithm Design
- Stochastic Processes and Optimization
- Applications in Operations Research
